# Correlation Geometry Under Doppler

## Core Idea

Correlation behavior under Doppler can be viewed geometrically as constructive and destructive accumulation regions.

## Motivation

Traditional synchronization explanations often focus only on equations. Here we visualize how correlation evolves spatially across Doppler conditions.

## Goals

- Visualize constructive regions
- Visualize destructive regions
- Study incremental gain behavior
- Analyze synchronization robustness
